NYC DOB Permits Issued

DM, NB & Alt-1 Permits Issued from 2008 to 2021

Every year the Department of Buildings issues thousands of construction permits. Permits used in this analysis are as follows:

DM - Demolition Permits, NB - New Building Permits & Alt-1 - Alteration 1 Permits

Each of these permits allows for certain types of construction to occur: DM allows the full demolition of a building, NB allows the construction of a new building and Alt-1 allows the change of egress and/or occupancy of an existing building. Of course these types of construction must meet the NYC Construction Code requirements.
